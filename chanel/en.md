## 品牌介绍

Chanel (French: Chanel, pronounced: [ʃanɛl]) is a top luxury goods company founded by Coco Chanel in 1910. It originated in France. It was originally a small shop specializing in customized hats. It later evolved into a cross-disciplinary media group that integrates clothing, leather bags, shoes, perfumes, accessories, fashion exhibitions, etc. In 1912, when the brand was founded, Coco Chanel chose the company's address in the fashion capital - Paris, France. After several setbacks, Coco moved the store to No. 31 rue Cambon next to Place Vendôme, which has since become Chanel's head office. As a pioneering female fashion designer, Coco broke the design norms of women's clothing in the 19th century by removing elements such as tutu skirts, evening gowns, heavy jewelry, and exposed breasts, and inventing her own clothing instead, such as women's suit trousers, elegant trousers, and more. Elegant little black dresses, smart Chanel short tops, knee-length skirts, etc. These clothes are knitted with wool, equipped with black trimmings and gold buttons, and are paired with large pearl necklaces. Finally, they are sprayed with Chanel No. 5 perfume as the finishing touch, and they have since led the aesthetics of the fashion circle. Chanel is currently a private company owned by Alain Wertheimer and Gérard Wertheimer, the grandsons of Pierre Wertheimer, an early business partner of founder Coco Chanel.
