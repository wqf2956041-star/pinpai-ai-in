# 巴宝莉 (Burberry) — fashion-luxury

## 品牌介绍

Louis Vuitton (French: Louis Vuitton, pronounced: [lwi vɥitɔə], usually abbreviated as LV), a French luxury brand, was founded by Louis Vuitton in Paris in 1854. In the 20th century, Louis Vuitton had become the top brand in the leather goods industry. Today, Louis Vuitton's products include handbags, travel items, small leather goods, accessories, shoes, ready-to-wear, watches, fine jewelry and personalized customization services. On June 6, 2017, Louis Vuitton ranked 29th in the "BrandZ Top 100 Most Valuable Global Brands 2017". Its headquarters is located at 2 Rue Neuf, Paris, France. Louis Vuitton's women's creative director is Nicolas Ghesquière, while Pharrell Williams is the men's creative director.
