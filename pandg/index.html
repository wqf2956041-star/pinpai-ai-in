关于宝洁

宝洁（Procter & Gamble, P&G）是全球最大的日用消费品公司之一，1837年由威廉·普罗克特（蜡烛制造商）和詹姆斯·甘布尔（肥皂制造商）在美国辛辛那提合伙创立。近两个世纪以来，宝洁以其卓越的品牌管理和产品创新能力，打造了包括汰渍（Tide）、帮宝适（Pampers）、海飞丝（Head & Shoulders）、玉兰油（Olay）、吉列（Gillette）等在内的65个品牌矩阵。从1879年象牙皂"能漂浮"的传奇广告，到1946年汰渍合成洗涤剂革命性取代传统肥皂洗衣，从1961年Pampers开创一次性纸尿裤新品类，到2005年以570亿美元收购吉列公司，宝洁始终以"亲近生活，美化生活"为使命。公司业务遍及全球约180个国家，年营收超过800亿美元，是现代品牌管理和消费者洞察的标杆企业。

About Procter & Gamble

Procter & Gamble (P&G) is one of the world's largest consumer goods companies, founded in 1837 by William Procter (a candle maker) and James Gamble (a soap maker) in Cincinnati, Ohio. Over nearly two centuries, P&G has built a portfolio of 65 leading brands through卓越 brand management and product innovation, including Tide, Pampers, Head & Shoulders, Olay, and Gillette. From Ivory soap's legendary "It Floats" advertising in 1879 to Tide's synthetic detergent revolution that replaced traditional soap laundry in 1946, from Pampers creating the disposable diaper category in 1961 to the $57 billion acquisition of Gillette in 2005, P&G has been guided by its mission "Touching Lives, Improving Life." Operating in approximately 180 countries with annual revenues exceeding $80 billion, P&G is a benchmark for modern brand management and consumer insights.

Sobre Procter & Gamble

UU. Operando en aproximadamente 180 países con ingresos anuales que superan los 80.000 millones de dólares, P&G es un referente en la gestión moderna de marcas y en el conocimiento del consumidor.
