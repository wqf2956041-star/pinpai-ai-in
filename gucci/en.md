# 圣罗兰 (Saint Laurent) — general

## 品牌介绍

Gucci is a brand of Kering Group. It was founded in 1921 and is headquartered in Florence, Italy. It mainly sells leather products, clothing, shoes and hats, cosmetics and perfumes. Founder Guccio Gucci opened his eponymous handmade luggage workshop in Florence in 1921, which initially started with high-end leather goods and horse gear. In 1947, the bamboo handbag was launched, which became the brand's iconic item with its unique bamboo handle design. In 1953, it opened its first overseas store in New York, becoming one of the first Italian luxury brands to enter the U.S. market; it launched branded shoes in the same year. In 1955, the Horsebit 1955 series of handbags was launched, with a horsebit design that paid tribute to the equestrian tradition and became another classic. In 1972, the first clothing store was opened in New York. In 1981, the Flora flower series was launched. Gucci is famous for its classic designs such as the double G logo, green and red striped webbing and bamboo handles, and is regarded as a symbol of Italy's economic miracle. In 2006, the Lavender Guccissima series was launched. Joined the International Fur-Free Alliance in 2017. In 2019, sales exceeded 10 billion euros, and it was ranked 33rd on the Interbrand Top 100 Global Brands list. In 2020, Forbes ranked 31st among the top 100 most valuable global brands. In 2024, it will be selected as the 129th among the top 500 most valuable global brands.
