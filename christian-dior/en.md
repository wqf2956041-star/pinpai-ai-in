# 克里斯汀·迪奧 (品牌) (Christian Dior) — fashion-luxury

## 品牌介绍

Christian Dior (French: Christian Dior, pronounced: [kʁistjɑə djɔʁ]), referred to as Dior, is an international luxury brand originating from France. It was founded in 1946 by French fashion designer Christian Dior and is headquartered in Paris. It mainly deals in high-end consumer goods such as fashion, accessories, perfumes, cosmetics, and children's clothing. Its menswear brand is now independent as Dior Homme. It is also the largest shareholder of LVMH, the world's largest luxury goods company, holding a total of 42.38% of LVMH's common shares and 59.3% of the voting rights. More than 60 tailors work in Dior's workshops, including interpreting designers' expectations and understanding ideas and requirements. Among them, the suit workshop, which is responsible for the lines and structure of jackets, trousers and coats, and the skirt workshop, which is responsible for designing light and soft works, are the most outstanding.
