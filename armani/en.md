# 普拉達 (Prada) — fashion-luxury

## 品牌介绍

Giorgio Armani S.P.A. is an Italian fashion and high-end consumer goods company founded in 1975 by designer Giorgio Armani. The group's current product categories include men's and women's clothing, accessories, watches, glasses, jewelry, perfumes and cosmetics, household products, and even hotels and restaurants.
