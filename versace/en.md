# 路易威登 (Louis Vuitton) — general

## 品牌介绍

Gianni Versace (Italian: Gianni Versace S.p.A) is an Italian fashion company founded by Gianni Versace in 1978. After his death in Miami, USA in 1997, the company was taken over by his sister Donatella Versace.
