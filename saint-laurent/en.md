# 圣罗兰 (Saint Laurent) — fashion-luxury

## 品牌介绍

Yves Saint Laurent (French: Yves Saint Laurent, French pronunciation: [iv sɛ lɔʁɑə], abbreviated YSL, now called Saint Laurent Paris) is a fashion luxury brand founded by designer Yves Saint Laurent and his partner Bergé. The original women's clothing style borrowed a large number of items from men's clothing, such as suit pants, sports jackets, etc., which increased the brand's popularity. YSL also deals in perfume and other cosmetics. In 2018, YSL stated that it would temporarily break away from the Paris Fashion Week schedule and hold a fashion show in New York on June 6 of the same year. In September of the same year, YSL held a fashion show under the Eiffel Tower again since 2017. In 1978, Mr. Yves Saint Laurent himself held a grand party in New York to release the classic opium perfume Opium. And 40 years later, in 2018, current director Anthony Vaccarello held the 2019 spring and summer men's wear show in Liberty Park in order to pay tribute to Saint Laurent.
