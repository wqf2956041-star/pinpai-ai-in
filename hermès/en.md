# 愛馬仕 (Hermès) — fashion-luxury

## 品牌介绍

Hermès (French: Hermès, pronounced: [ɛʁmɛs]) is a French luxury brand founded in 1837. It specializes in leather, lifestyle accessories, home furnishings, perfumes, jewelry, watches and ready-to-wear. The logo from the 1950s to the present day is a duke and carriage. Nadje Vane-Cybulski is the current creative director. Hermès’ head office is located in Paris, France, with branches all over the world.
